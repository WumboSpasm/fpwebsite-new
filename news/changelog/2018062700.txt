Flashpoint 3.2 - Shock and Awe

Flashpoint now supports Shockwave titles. Shockwave content is a bit more tempremental than your traditional Flash stuff, so don't expect new games too fast. If you want just Shockwave titles, you can sort by Platform.

We've added an "OldProjectors" folder that'll hold onto the older Flash projector versions for games that need them. They serve no purpose right now but hopefully will in the future.

Updated the Reset Flashpoint batch file.
