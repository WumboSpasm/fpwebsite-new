Games that are notably extreme in violence or sexual content are now "Hidden", but "Show Hidden Games" is on by default. If you want to hide the extreme games, you can turn off "Show Hidden Games" in the "View" menu. Note that the threshold of what games should and shouldn't be hidden is different for everyone; you can hide games yourself by right-clicking a game and clicking the "Hide" checkbox, then clicking OK.
